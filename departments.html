What is UG Program
A UG program, or Undergraduate Program, is the first level of higher education after high school (12th grade) that leads to a bachelor's degree, providing foundational knowledge in a specific field like Arts (BA), Science (BSc), Commerce (BCom), or Engineering (B.Tech/BE), typically lasting 3-4 years and preparing students for entry-level careers or further study.
What is PG Program
A PG (Postgraduate) program is an advanced course of study pursued after completing a bachelor's degree, designed for deeper specialization in a field, career advancement, or research, encompassing Master's degrees (MA, MSc, MBA), Doctoral degrees (PhDs), and shorter postgraduate diplomas or certificates. These programs build on undergraduate knowledge, offering in-depth skills, professional development, and higher academic credentials.
